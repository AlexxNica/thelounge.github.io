Always connected

The Shout server never sleeps. Pick up where you left off when you sign in.

Responsive

The interface is responsive. That means that the client works as good on your smartphone as it does on your desktop.

Cross Platform

It doesn't matter if you use Windows, Mac OSX or Linux. Shout just works.

Multiple Users

Support for multiple user accounts. Share your server with your friends!

Use multiple devices

You can stay connected with multiple devices at the same time.
